My Hobbies


Playing Video Games

I've always loved playing video games, and for a long time, I spent most of my free time immersed in competitive games like League of Legends, Valorant, and CS:GO. The idea of constantly improving my skills kept me coming back for more. But over time, I began to realize that the stress and addictiveness that come with these games weren’t healthy for me. The pressure to constantly perform and the emotional toll of losing started to outweigh the enjoyment.

Now, I’ve shifted my focus to playing more laid-back JRPGs. I find the slower pace, immersive storytelling, and emphasis on personal growth much more relaxing, and it’s helped me enjoy gaming without the intense pressure that once dominated my experience.
